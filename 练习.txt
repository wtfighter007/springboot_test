With the improvement of living standards, holiday is occupying a more and more promient
position in Chinese people's life. In the past, making a living takes most of people's time, 
which gives them rare chance to go off  on a trip. However, tourism has undergone a rapid
growth in China for the past several years. The prosperity of economy and the emergence of
the affluent middle class trigger an unprecedented tourism boom. Not only does domestic traveling
become common, but traveling abroad is also enjoying an increasing popularity among Chinese people.
 During the National Day holidays in 2016, tourism consumption amounts to more than 400 billion yuan.
According to the statistical data by the World Trade Organization, China will have become the world's
largest tourism country by 2020, and she will be also see the fastest growth in overseas traveling
expenditure in the next few years.
